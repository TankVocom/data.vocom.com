VOCOM / COMPANY PROFILE

Twenty-five years of telecom, still self-funded and privately held

VOCOM's mission is to bring innovative, high-quality telecommunications to its partner carriers and communities around the world.

SINCE 1998

A facilities-based carrier, not a reseller

Vocom International Telecommunications, Inc. was founded in 1998 with offices in the USA and Taiwan. It operates as a facilities-based provider under license from the FCC's International Bureau, with partners spanning the full range from PTT and Tier 1 carriers down to Tier 2 IXCs and LECs.

The company is entirely self-funded, running a modern facility with current-generation telecom equipment, supported by an in-house team of telecom engineers trained to hold Quality of Service levels across every carrier route.

Scope of operations

Large on the US West Coast, focused across the Pacific

VOCOM is one of the largest privately held telecommunications carriers on the West Coast of the United States, with core services concentrated in cross-Pacific destinations — China, Hong Kong, and Taiwan carry particular emphasis.

Market leader in Asia termination

VOCOM's IDD business leads the market for Asia termination out of Los Angeles.

Competitive beyond Asia

Active and competitive routes in the Middle East, Europe, and the Americas.

Deep interconnection

Extensive local, national, and international interconnects through legacy TDM and modern VoIP/NGN.

Business development, 1998–2026

Every PoP and business launch, in order

The full history behind the network map — where each business line started and where the PoPs came online.

  • 1998

    VOCOM established, HQ in Los Angeles. Voice wholesale service. First data center in L.A. LA PoP online.

  • 2006

    DATA business launched: IPLC and IP Transit services. Hong Kong PoP and Taipei PoP online.

  • 2012

    Mobile Data, eSIM, and SMS business launched. Singapore PoP and Tokyo PoP online.

  • 2017

    Fiber Optic Cables business launched. Manila, Kuala Lumpur, and Shenzhen PoPs online.

  • 2020

    MPLS VPN and DIA services. ICT and Anti-DDoS services. Bangkok, UAE/Fujairah, Ashburn, Shanghai, and Mexico PoPs online.

  • 2024

    Tokyo new PoP online. Ho Chi Minh City PoP online. Jakarta PoP and San Jose PoP in progress.

  • 2026

    AI server business launched. Seoul, London, Sao Paulo, Toronto, and Sihanoukville PoPs online.
